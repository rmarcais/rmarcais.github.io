Motiv'

Let's do sport together

Motiv is a website to that allows you to meet people near you for a fun and motivating workout. So, ready to try the adventure?

Discover it now !

Features

In this section, you can find some features available in this version.

Create an account

The perfect way to start using Motiv' is to create an acount.The only information you need are a username, a password, your location, the sports you like and a biography (optional). That's all ! You're now a Motiv' user !

Meet friends !

Search for friends in the "Meet friends !" section! You can filter users by username, location (city and department) and sport. By clicking on the "show infos !" button, you can see all the selected user's information such as his reviews, his location (with a map), his events, his sports and his bio.

Search an join events !

If you want to do sport with other people, this section is made for you ! Indeed, you can see all the available events and filter them by (like users) title, location and sports !. If you want to see more details about an event, you can click the button "show infos !" and once you want to join it, just click "Join !". Good session !

About this project

I had the idea for Motiv' about 3 months ago, at the beginning of April 2022 when I wanted to go to the gym. I had no one to go with, which discouraged me most of the time. It was after a few sessions that I said to myself: "It would be so cool if I could go there with people who could then become my friends". It is from this moment that Motiv' was born. Afterwards, I thought it would be interesting to extend the concept to all sports, such as basketball, football or even dance! Because yes, all these activities are better together ;). Finally, I took advantage of the Portfolio project included in the curriculum of my school, Holberton school, to achieve the MVP.
